Welcome to the Sports Leisure Centre App, here you will find small snippets of news on day to day life here at the Gym as well as updates on any upcoming offers we may have to offer throughout the year.

What do we have to offer?

Instructions

(1)When using this part of the app always make sure you hit the show current tasks button. This will load any tasks from previous sessions. If you do not press this button before adding a new task you run the risk of losing old tasks.

(2)To Add a new Gym-Task all you have to do is press the add a new Gym-Task button and follow the promted screen pop ups it is as simple as that.

(3)When you have completed a task hit the checkbox beside it and the task will be scored out. The task may be completed but will remain in your list until completed tasks have been cleared or the individual task is cleared using its own delete button.

(4)To update a task simply click on the text box containing its description and you will be able to alter the text.
